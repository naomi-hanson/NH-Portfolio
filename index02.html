Bay Beans

Bay Beans is an app to help local college students find a cafe to study at or to hang out with friends, respecting personal aesthetics and specific needs.

The problem was trying to appeal to the aesthetic choices of the participants. What exactly would they look for in a cafe versus a restaurant.
